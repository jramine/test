Argumentry

Objectives of the Congress:

This congress will highlight the interplay between Management and Marketing in enhancing organizational performance, emphasizing the impact of ecological and digital transitions, and introduces the objectives of a conference focused on addressing these challenges.

Dialogue

The conference's primary focus is to foster meaningful dialogue among Marketing and Management professionals and researchers.

Resilience

The conference aims to address how companies can enhance their resilience during economic and health crises, while adapting to digital practices in the ever-evolving global landscape.

Strategies

The event intends to explore contemporary Management and Marketing strategies, especially concerning ecological and digital transitions, with an emphasis on realigning approaches for sustainability and enhanced performance.

Collaboration

The conference acts as a catalyst for scientific collaboration, bringing together experts in Marketing and Management to collectively advance knowledge and solutions for optimizing organizational performance.
